Conference - Atlanta, GA - June 22-23, 2010

Chris Jordan

Chris Jordan

Chris Jordan started Atlanta Insurance Live, a unique Internet-based insurance marketing platform built on WordPress. His interactive video blog offers an informative, and refreshing approach to insurance. His real-world application of WordPress has garnered the attention of his industry and beyond. The Insurance Journal, Advisor Today, Independent Agent Magazine, and INC Magazine have all featured his story.

Chris works closely with the insurance industry as a digital marketing consultant, and also helps individuals and small businesses develop a refined web presence using the WordPress platform.
