Promote your Products & Services

If you sell products or services to a WordPress-centric business audience you should seriously consider sponsoring or exhibiting at The Business Of WordPress Conference. This event targets business users who are currently using and/or plan to use WordPress for their businesses’ web presence and they are exactly the type of people who need the products and services you offer and who have the money to pay for them.

Additionally if you represent a products or online services vendor that targets business users of WordPress you can doubly benefit from sponsoring or exhibiting; you’ll not only reach attendees you’ll also reach our influential group of presenters and our service provider exhibitors as well.

Sponsorships at The Business Of WordPress are designed for those companies whose marketing goals demand that they generate that extra edge in visibility and leadership status within their market. Sponsorship opportunities are available to fit any budget, are on a first-come, first-served basis and are subject to change as the conference planning progresses.
